About Us

Moody is a social publishing platform that is home to a diverse array of stories, ideas, and perspectives. Published by our dynamic team of professional writers, popular topics on Moody range from lifestyle to technology, and from career advice to self care routines to productivity hacks.

Founder Pragati Mehra launched the platform 'Moody' in the year 2021, referring to it as revolutionary, with the ideology:

"Moody is not about who you are or whom you know, but about what you have to say."

Moody is a motive-driven content providing platform, with a holistic approach to increase people's well-being and resilience.

To help emphasize the importance and the significance of the content, the overall design of Moody is minimalistic, featuring lots of white space and limited formatting options. The idea is to keep the design simple, yet elegant; keeping distractions minimal and the reader consuming and interacting with content on-the-go.
