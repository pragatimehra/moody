Pragati Mehra

Founder & Content Strategist

A sophomore at Guru Tegh Bahadur Institute of Technology, Pragati Mehra is pursuing Bachelor's in Technology. Former Tryst, IIT-Delhi speaker, she believes hard work and perseverance always pay off. She works on to pursuing UI/UX & Full Stack as her career, but she is firmly opinionated that career should never obstruct one's passion.

With her talent, commitment, dedication, discipline and hard work, Pragati launched 'Moody', a social publishing platform that is home to a diverse array of stories, ideas, and perspectives. She launched the platform in 2021, referring to it as revolutionary, with the ideology:

"Moody is not about who you are or whom you know, but about what you have to say."

Moody is a motive-driven content providing platform, with a holistic approach to increase people's well-being and resilience. Although the platform has just been introduced, it has already started making it's impact with the growing outreach and immense audience engagement.
